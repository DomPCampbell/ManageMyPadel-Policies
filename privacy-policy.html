Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data

Effective date: 4 April 2026

1 Introduction

ManageMyPadel ("the App") is developed and operated by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that information. By using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2 Information We Collect

The App collects only the information that is strictly necessary for it to function. When you add a padel venue booking site to be managed, you provide:

  • Your login credentials (username/email and password) for each third-party padel venue booking platform you choose to connect.

The App does not collect your name, email address, phone number, location data, payment information, or any other personal data beyond the venue booking credentials you provide.

3 How Your Data Is Stored

All credentials are stored locally on your device only. They are never transmitted to, or stored on, any server or cloud service operated by us.

In order to manage your bookings, the App transmits your credentials directly to the relevant third-party venue booking platform to log in on your behalf and retrieve your booking information. This is the same data transmission that would occur if you logged in to the venue's website yourself. Your credentials are not sent anywhere else.

Because your stored data remains entirely on your device, only you (or anyone with physical access to your device) can access it. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, your stored credentials will be permanently deleted.

4 Third-Party Sharing

ManageMyPadel does not share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your data to third parties. The App contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5 How Your Credentials Are Used

The venue login credentials you provide are used solely for the purpose of logging in to the respective padel venue booking platforms on your behalf. Once logged in, the App retrieves your booking details from the venue's platform so that it can display and manage them for you. Credentials are not used for any other purpose.

6 Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device, including using a screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date.

We strongly recommend that you use unique passwords for each venue booking platform and consider using a password manager.

7 Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • View or update your stored credentials within the App.
  • Delete individual venue credentials from the App at any time.
  • Remove all data by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage.

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, including the right to access, rectification, and erasure of your personal data. Since all data is stored locally and not processed by us, these rights are effectively exercised through the controls described above.

8 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided credentials through the App, please contact us so we can advise on how to remove the data from the device.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
